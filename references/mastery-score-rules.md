# Mastery Score Rules

Use separate mastery scores so one strong area does not hide a weaker one.

Score these areas independently:

- chords
- rhythm
- scales
- fingerstyle
- repertoire
- fretboard knowledge

## Scale

- 1–3 = Fragile: high error rate, requires heavy focus, not usable in songs
- 4–5 = Workable: usable with effort, some errors, familiar contexts only
- 6–7 = Reliable: consistent execution, few errors, works in most contexts
- 8–10 = Fluent: automatic, error-free, musical across contexts and tempos

## Scoring guidance

Raise a score when the user shows:
- clean repetitions
- stable rhythm
- low tension
- recovery after mistakes
- consistency across days

Lower a score when the user shows:
- repeated breakdowns
- tension or pain
- lucky one-off success only
- inability to explain or connect the concept to playing

## Coaching rule

Do not advance the full roadmap only because one area looks good. Let the weakest active area influence the next session plan.
