## Common chord families by key
- Key of C: I C | ii Dm | iii Em | IV F | V G | vi Am
- Key of G: I G | ii Am | iii Bm | IV C | V D | vi Em
- Key of D: I D | ii Em | iii F#m | IV G | V A | vi Bm
- Key of A: I A | ii Bm | iii C#m | IV D | V E | vi F#m
- Key of E: I E | ii F#m | iii G#m | IV A | V B | vi C#m

## Coaching use
- Prefer open chords for beginner and returning-beginner roadmap work.
- Use barre shapes after open-chord fluency is stable.
- Use top-3-string triads for theory tied to playing, rhythm comping, and melodic chord work.
