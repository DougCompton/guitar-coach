# Default Roadmaps

Create these roadmap files if no roadmap files exist in the working folder. Each roadmap should connect technique, repertoire, and theory directly to the instrument.

## Beginner
Filename: `roadmap-beginner.md`

1. Open-string reset and posture
2. Basic open chords: Em, Am, C, G, D
3. Steady quarter-note and eighth-note strumming
4. Clean chord changes in 2-chord loops
5. Major scale pattern plus intervals on one string
6. First complete song with open chords
7. Chord construction from familiar open shapes
8. Review and tempo growth

## Intermediate
Filename: `roadmap-intermediate.md`

1. Barre-chord refresh and fret-hand efficiency
2. Strumming control with accents and syncopation
3. Alternate picking and position shifts
4. Minor pentatonic phrasing and bends
5. Triads on top strings
6. Groove playing and rhythm precision with a metronome
7. Song study with full-section transitions
8. Review and consolidation

## Fingerstyle
Filename: `roadmap-fingerstyle.md`

1. Thumb-index-middle-ring assignment reset
2. Travis-picking basics
3. Alternating bass with steady treble notes
4. Chord melody fragments on top strings
5. Right-hand independence and pattern changes
6. Fingerstyle arrangement of a simple tune
7. Triads inside fingerstyle chord shapes
8. Review and repertoire polish

## Celtic
Filename: `roadmap-celtic.md`

1. DADGAD or standard-tuning drone concepts
2. Modal scale shapes and ornament basics
3. Open-string drones with melody fragments
4. Reel and jig rhythm feel
5. Hammer-ons, pull-offs, and cuts in context
6. Celtic-style accompaniment patterns
7. Learn a simple traditional tune in ASCII tab
8. Review and phrasing polish

## Rock
Filename: `roadmap-rock.md`

1. Power chords on E and A strings with correct muting
2. Palm muting and pick-attack control
3. Root-5 power chord movement and basic rock rhythm patterns
4. Down-stroke riffing and string crossing at steady tempo
5. Minor pentatonic scale box 1 and first simple lick
6. Bending basics: half-step and whole-step bends in tune
7. Riff-based song study using power chords and pentatonic ideas
8. Review and tone: dynamics, muting clarity, and rhythmic tightness

## Blues
Filename: `roadmap-blues.md`

1. 12-bar blues structure with open E, A, and B7 chords
2. Shuffle feel and swing eighth-note rhythm
3. Minor pentatonic box 1 and the blues note (b5)
4. Call-and-response phrasing over a 12-bar form
5. Expressive bending and vibrato in context
6. Simple blues lick library: turnarounds, rakes, and slides
7. Playing a complete 12-bar blues from start to finish musically
8. Review and improvisation: making choices, leaving space, and phrasing

## Country
Filename: `roadmap-country.md`

1. Open chord vocabulary with capo: common keys on capo 2 and capo 4
2. Alternating bass pick: thumb-driven bass note with pick or fingers on treble strings
3. Basic hybrid picking: pick and middle finger together on adjacent strings
4. Chicken pickin' introduction: muted snap on treble strings with ring or middle finger
5. Pedal-steel-style bends: whole-step bends in tune on the B and G strings
6. Country rhythm feel: boom-chick pattern and shuffle subdivision
7. Simple country lead over a I–IV–V progression
8. Review and taste: clean tone, dynamics, and phrasing space
