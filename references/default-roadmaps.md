# Default Roadmaps

Create these roadmap files if no roadmap files exist in the working folder. Each roadmap should connect technique, repertoire, and theory directly to the instrument.

## Beginner
Filename: `roadmap-beginner.md`

1. Open-string reset and posture [difficulty:1]
2. Basic open chords: Em, Am, C, G, D [difficulty:2]
3. Steady quarter-note and eighth-note strumming [difficulty:2]
4. Clean chord changes in 2-chord loops [difficulty:3]
5. Major scale pattern plus intervals on one string [difficulty:3]
6. First complete song with open chords [difficulty:3]
7. Chord construction from familiar open shapes [difficulty:4]
8. Review and tempo growth [difficulty:3]

## Country
Filename: `roadmap-country.md`

1. Open chord vocabulary with capo: common keys on capo 2 and capo 4 [difficulty:2]
2. Alternating bass pick: thumb-driven bass note with pick or fingers on treble strings [difficulty:3]
3. Basic hybrid picking: pick and middle finger together on adjacent strings [difficulty:3]
4. Chicken pickin' introduction: muted snap on treble strings with ring or middle finger [difficulty:4]
5. Pedal-steel-style bends: whole-step bends in tune on the B and G strings [difficulty:4]
6. Country rhythm feel: boom-chick pattern and shuffle subdivision [difficulty:3]
7. Simple country lead over a I–IV–V progression [difficulty:4]
8. Review and taste: clean tone, dynamics, and phrasing space [difficulty:3]
